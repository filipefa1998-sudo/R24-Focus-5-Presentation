The Problem → Solution

WHY WE
BUILT THIS

Ward councils without a tracker are flying blind. Paper lists get lost. Spreadsheets go stale. People get forgotten.

😓

❌ Before Focus Five

Scattered paper notes. No visibility between meetings. Investigators lost track of. No one knew who needed a visit, a text, or a prayer.

🔍

❌ No Accountability

Hard to know which family member was assigned to whom. Progress only visible on Sunday, if someone remembered to report it.

✅

✅ With Focus Five

Every investigator and returning member visible in real-time. Every invitation, attendance, and milestone logged and tracked month-by-month.

🏆

✅ Results We've Seen

Ward council meetings got shorter and sharper. Leaders know exactly who needs attention. Covenant path milestones are celebrated together.

Feature Overview

EVERYTHING
IN ONE PLACE

Nine powerful features that work together to keep your ward's missionary efforts organized and inspired.

1
👥
Investigators & Returning Members
Two separate lists — investigators and returning members — each with names, emoji avatars, assigned family, personal goals, and notes.
Core
2
📊
Monthly Activity Tracker
Quarter-by-quarter progress grid. Click any indicator to log it. Color-coded by category. Navigate by quarter and see the full year at a glance.
Core
3
📖
The Covenant Path
When someone is baptized, they automatically move to this section. Track all ordinances (Aaronic Priesthood → Endowment) and self-reliance classes.
Milestone
4
📋
Ward Council Report
One-click report generation. Shows all activity this month, covenant path progress, who needs attention, and upcoming events. Printable.
Leadership
5
🙏
Prayer List
A dedicated, always-visible prayer roll. Add names, notes, and see how many days they've been on the list. Synced to ward council reports.
Spiritual
6
📅
Ward Event Calendar
Full calendar with list and month views. Mark events as "invite-ready" to surface them in reports. Share event details via one tap. Add events directly to calendar days.
Planning
7
🔴
Needs-Attention Alerts
Auto-detects people with names but zero activity logged this month. Surfaces them in the ward council report so no one is ever forgotten.
Smart
8
💾
Real-Time Firebase Sync
Every change saves instantly to the cloud. Multiple leaders can view simultaneously. Export/import JSON for backups. Dark mode included.
Tech
9
📱
Mobile-First Design
Works perfectly on phones and tablets. Share event details using your phone's native share sheet. No app to download — just a link.
