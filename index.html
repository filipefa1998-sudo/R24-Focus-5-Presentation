The Foundation
Matthew 18 : 11 – 14
"…If a man have a hundred sheep, and one of them be gone astray, doth he not leave the ninety and nine, and goeth into the mountains, and seeketh that which is gone astray?

…Even so it is not the will of your Father that one of these little ones should perish."
Every name in our tracker is that one lost sheep.
The ward — all 99 — work together to bring them home.
How We Apply It
FOCUS ON THE ONE.
MINISTER TO THE 99.

👤 Investigators

Of everyone we're teaching, who is closest to progressing? That person gets the focused energy of the whole ward — invitations, prayer, visits, events.

💙 Returning Members

Of all our less-active members, who is closest to coming back? One name. One family. The whole ward channels attention there first — then shifts when they return.

🔄 The Funnel

Once the one comes back to the fold, focus shifts to the next person. The tracker makes this obvious — most indicators = closest to progressing.

📊 Why It Works

Our ministering efforts now have a focus. When the Focus Person is not available, we minister to the 99 — assignments keep everyone connected and no one forgotten.
